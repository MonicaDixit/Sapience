SOA WebServices

Sapience’s unique approach to the market place is demonstrated clearly with our dedicated practice that focuses on Service Oriented Architecture (SOA); the conventional rules that govern software programming have not always eased communication between applications and systems. Sapience works to help our clients break down functions into services, and end the day of the monolithic application forever.
Our understanding and experience in the space helps our clients map a clear path to recombine services where possible, while maximizing reuse of existing services. Sapience believes SOA is not a question of if, but when for organizations in the new economy; in order to operate in a more agile fashion and cut fixed costs, the business needs must drive IT projects in every sense of integration.
Helping Our Clients Breakdown Barriers Between Applications, Speed Systems Integration, and Lower Overall Cost. Sapience’s SOA Practice is built on many years of successful client work with web services, enterprise integration projects, and our SOA-specific client engagements. The mission of our SOA Practice is to work with our clients to help consolidate and integrate numerous technologies in the most cost-effective manner possible. Interoperability and Accelerating Your Business is Our Goal
Our Functional SOA Consultants work with our clients to identify the proper architecture, management, and governance standards across the business ecosystem.
