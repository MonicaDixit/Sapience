Business Intellligence

At Sapience, we recognize that Business Intelligence and Data Warehouse (BI/DW) initiatives are among the forefront of top priorities for organizations today. For many organizations, Business Intelligence and Data Warehousing are not new initiatives; but optimization and expansion of capabilities are the next area of focus.

For many organizations, a focus on BI/DW is an ongoing process to address data quality, user education, and in some cases, standardization of the existing infrastructure.

In recent years, technological advancements have made significant improvements to better enable clients to gather and store data. IT frameworks have evolved in such a way that many organizations gather and store data in multiple sources. Many companies, however, lack the resources to convert this data into useful information that can be used for business decisions.

Sapience's Business Intelligence Practice specializes in helping clients navigate through their enterprise-wide IT Infrastructure, utilizing "Best Practices" to create a roadmap to realize the full benefits of their investment.
